Stretches & Core

Do before breakfast daily — activates circulation, wakes core, reduces morning stiffness. Every day including Sunday. 5 exercises × 2 min each.

🔄
1. Neck Rolls + Shoulder Shrugs
2 min
Roll neck full circle — 5× clockwise, 5× anticlockwise. Then shrug shoulders to ears, hold 3 sec, release. Releases overnight neck tension. Good if you wake up foggy.
🙆
2. Full-Body Overhead Stretch
2 min
Feet shoulder-width. Interlace fingers, push arms up, palms to ceiling. Inhale up, exhale release. Hold 10 sec, 5 reps. Opens chest — especially important for bronchitis, improves lung capacity.
Lung health ↑
↔️
3. Standing Side Bends
2 min
Slide hand down thigh toward knee as you bend sideways — stretch opposite side of waist. Hold 5 sec, switch. 10 per side. Directly stretches obliques (belly sides).
Waist ↓
🔃
4. Standing Torso Twists
2 min
Feet shoulder-width, arms straight at shoulder height. Twist upper body left (hold 2 sec), return, then right. 15 per side. Directly rotates core muscles — tightens belly even before weight is lost.
🐱
5. Cat-Cow Spinal Stretch
2 min
On all fours (hands under shoulders, knees under hips). Inhale: arch back down, look up (cow). Exhale: round spine up (cat). 10 slow reps. Mobilises spine, activates deep core. Best move for desk workers.
Spine + core

5 days/week · 2–3 hrs after a meal · Can do same days as treadmill at different time. Start: 2 sets. By Wk 4: 3 sets. By Wk 8: feels easy.

🏋️
Plank Hold
30–60 sec2–3 sets
Forearms on floor, body straight, core tight. Hold 30 sec (Wk 1–2) → 45 sec (Wk 3–4) → 60 sec (Wk 5+). Most efficient belly-flattening exercise. Works transverse abdominis (deep layer).
🚴
Bicycle Crunches
15 per side2–3 sets
Lie on back, hands behind head. Alternate elbow to opposite knee in cycling motion. Slow and controlled. Works obliques + rectus abdominis simultaneously — highest belly-fat relevance of any crunch variant.
🦵
Leg Raises
10–15 reps2–3 sets
Lie flat. Lift both legs to 90°, slowly lower without touching floor. Lower abs + hip flexors. Best exercise for lower belly pouch specifically. Start with knees bent if needed in Wk 1.
🧎
Seated Knee Pulls
15 per side2 sets
Sit on edge of chair/bed, lean back slightly. Pull one knee to chest, return. Alternate. Works the same lower belly area as leg raises but lower intensity — good for days when energy is low.

5 minutes · any time — especially on non-core nights. Activates parasympathetic nervous system, lowers cortisol (belly fat driver), improves sleep quality.

Diaphragmatic Breathing — 5 min
Lie flat or sit comfortably. One hand on chest, one on belly. Inhale for 4 counts — belly should rise, chest should NOT. Exhale for 6 counts. 10 cycles. The extended exhale activates vagus nerve and lowers cortisol within minutes.
🎯 Cortisol = main visceral belly fat driver. This breathing lowers it directly.
4-7-8 Breathing for Sleep
Before sleep. Inhale 4 counts → hold 7 counts → exhale 8 counts. Repeat 4 cycles. Activates sleep onset. Best on nights when you had a late treadmill session and the body is still warm.
