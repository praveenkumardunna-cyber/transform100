Session structure
01
Warm-up — 4 min at 4–5 km/h
Don't start at 7 km/h cold. Airways need warm-up time with bronchitis history.
02
Main session — 7 km/h for 28–30 min
Breathing elevated but conversational — should say a short sentence without gasping. This is moderate-brisk walk, not run.
03
Cool-down — 3–5 min at 4 km/h
Slow gradually. Never stop abruptly. Keep moving until heart rate settles.
04
Post-session — room-temp water only
No cold water. Throat tightening risk. Sip slowly. No standing in AC airflow while breathing heavily.

warning
Bronchitis rules — non-negotiable
Any wheeze = slow belt immediately. Don't push through chest tightness or breathing difficulty beyond normal exertion. This is non-negotiable.
Room-temp water only after session. Never cold water — throat tightening risk.
No direct AC airflow on face during or immediately after. Keep room ventilated.
If genuinely can't do 35 min: do 15 min at any pace. Never zero. One skip becomes two, two becomes the new pattern.
Best timing options
⭐
6:00 PM — optimal (default)
Peak body temperature, glycogen loaded after lunch, 30 min after evening snack. Best performance window.
✓
5 PM (pre-snack) — if schedule shifted
Have evening snack after treadmill instead. Works fine.
✓
7 PM — late arrival
Dinner pushed to 8 PM. Strictly done by 8:30 PM still. Acceptable, not ideal.
⚠️
After 7:30 PM — avoid
Dinner cannot finish by 8:30 PM. Do 20-min walk instead on these days.
