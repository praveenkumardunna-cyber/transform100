Morning Drink Protocol
Before everything else
Methi-Ginger-Turmeric-Amla Drink

This one morning habit does more for your HbA1c, bloating, gut health than any single food choice at your meals.

Recipe — your everyday default
1
Soak 1 tsp methi seeds overnight
In a small glass of water. Next morning: drink the soaked water and chew the seeds.
2
Boil crushed fresh ginger 2-3 min
3-4 small pieces. Let cool slightly to warm (not hot).
3
Add turmeric + pinch black pepper
1/4 tsp turmeric. Black pepper is non-optional — piperine boosts curcumin absorption by ~2000%.
4
Stir in 20ml amla juice or half lemon
Drink warm. No honey. No sugar. 8 kcal total.
Never add honey — added sugar every morning works against HbA1c and weight loss goals daily.
Why each ingredient earns its place
Methi seeds
Strongest evidence
Reduces post-meal glucose spikes by 13-20% in clinical studies. Your HbA1c anchor.
Fresh ginger
4 goals
Digestion, bloating, anti-inflammatory, bronchitis support. Fresh only — powder is inferior.
Turmeric 1/4 tsp
Anti-inflammatory
Anti-inflammatory, post-TB lung support, blood sugar. Works only with black pepper.
Black pepper — pinch
Non-optional
Piperine boosts curcumin absorption by ~2000%. No pepper = turmeric barely works.
Amla / Lemon
Free Vitamin C
Vitamin C, immunity, zero sugar. Amla has 20x more Vitamin C than an orange.
Rotation options
Option A (default): Methi + amla — 5 days/week minimum. Strongest HbA1c evidence.
Option B: Jeera water — 1 tsp cumin boiled 5 min. Good for digestion. 1-2x/week variety.
Option C: Ajwain + saunf water — specifically for bloating/gas days. 1/2 tsp each, boiled.
Option D: Cinnamon water — 1/2 tsp or 1 stick steeped warm. Pleasant tasting, mild insulin support.
