12 Mandatory Daily Actions

Habit-based weight loss outperforms calorie-counting by 2-3x over 12 weeks.

1
2 glasses warm water - 8:00 AM
Before phone, before food. You lose 0.5-1L overnight. Morning grogginess is 60-70% dehydration.
2
Morning drink - methi + ginger + turmeric + amla
Warm. Empty stomach. 15 min before breakfast. Methi reduces post-meal glucose spikes by 13-20%.
3
10-minute morning stretch
5 exercises before breakfast. Activates circulation, wakes core, boosts BDNF for mental clarity.
4
1 glass water 20 min before every meal
All three meals, every day. 2015 Oxford study: pre-meal water = 44% greater weight loss over 12 weeks.
5
Protein first at every meal - always
Egg curry before idli. Curd before pesarattu. Protein-first reduces post-meal glucose spike by 28-38%.
6
Rasam first before rice - every lunch
Liquid pre-load reduces total calorie intake by 75-100 kcal per meal without conscious restriction.
7
10-min walk after breakfast
Within 30 min. Indoors counts. Reduces post-breakfast glucose by 12-22%.
8
10-min walk after lunch
Eliminates 3 PM crash. Reduces postprandial glucose by up to 22%.
9
Treadmill - 7 km/h, 35 min, 6 PM
~302 kcal burn. 5-6 days. If can't do 35 min, do 15 min. Never zero.
10
10-min walk after dinner
Most impactful 10 minutes. Reduces tomorrow's fasting glucose by 4-6 mg/dL. Your HbA1c is being written right now.
11
Stop eating by 8:30 PM - hard stop
Late eating raises fasting blood glucose by 4-8 mg/dL next morning. Evening insulin sensitivity is lowest.
12
Sleep by 10:30 PM - screens off 10 PM
GH peaks in first 90 min of sleep - this is when treadmill converts to fat loss. One late night adds 200+ kcal the next day.
