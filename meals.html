These are not suggestions
Plate rules — follow all, not the convenient ones
Habit-based weight loss outperforms calorie-counting by 2–3× over 12 weeks.
1
Protein first at every meal — always
Eat the protein component before the carb dish. Egg curry before idli. Curd before pesarattu. Protein-first ordering reduces post-breakfast glucose spike by 28–38%.
2
Rasam or thin dal FIRST before rice
1 small bowl before rice — every single time. Liquid pre-load reduces total calorie intake by 75–100 kcal per meal without conscious restriction.
3
Rice: ¾ cup cooked (120g) — same bowl daily
Use the same measuring bowl every day so you stop measuring by eye. Restaurant rice portions are typically 2–3× this. No second serving.
4
Rice-free 5 nights per week minimum
Evening insulin sensitivity is lowest — rice at 9 PM stores as fat more readily than rice at 1 PM. Same total carbs, earlier in the day.
5
Stop eating by 8:30 PM — hard stop
Late eating raises fasting blood glucose by 4–8 mg/dL next morning. Each night of 8:30 PM cutoff directly writes your HbA1c number downward.
6
Hidden calorie traps to avoid
Coconut chutney: cap 1.5 tbsp (30 kcal difference). Oil on tawa: 1 tsp per dosa. Ghee roast/butter dosa: Sunday only. If hungry after portion: add sambar or egg — never a 4th idli or 3rd dosa.
