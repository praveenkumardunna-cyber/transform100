Why weekly, not daily?

Body weight fluctuates 1–2.5 kg daily from water, food, hormones, and sodium. Daily weighing shows noise not fat loss. Stanford study (n=1,042): weekly weighers lost 2× more than daily weighers over 12 months on the same plan. Weekly = signal. Daily = noise + anxiety.
